# Lunaria Dark

used for applications that don't support opacity (alpha blending). These
variables represent the Dodrantopaque (`0.75`), Semiopaque (`0.5`),
Quadrantopaque (`0.25`) and Octantopaque (`0.125`) colors of the background
chromatics.
